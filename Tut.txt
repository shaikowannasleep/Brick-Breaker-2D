Bilinear Interpolation (Ni suy t din):
Bilinear interpolation s dng mt php ni suy hai chiu, kt hp thng tin t bn im ln cn. Khi ni suy t din, gi tr ca mt im c tnh ton bng cch ly trung bnh c trng s ca cc gi tr ti cc im ln cn, vi trng s c tnh da trn khong cch t im ang xt n cc im ln cn. Phng php ny to ra mt kt qu mt m hn so vi point interpolation v gim hin tng pixelation. Bilinear interpolation thng c s dng cho hnh nh (textures) 2D.

Trilinear Interpolation (Ni suy t din 3D):
Trilinear interpolation l mt phng php ni suy ba chiu, thng c s dng cho cc ti nguyn 3D nh textures hoc d liu vertex. Khi ni suy t din 3D, gi tr ca mt im c tnh ton bng cch s dng php ni suy t din trn hai lp ca cc gi tr ln cn. u tin, n s dng php ni suy t din  tnh ton gi tr trn lp di (lower layer) v lp trn (upper layer) ca cc im ln cn. Sau , n s dng php ni suy tuyn tnh trn cc gi tr  tnh  tnh ton gi tr cui cng ca im ang xt. Trilinear interpolation cung cp kt qu mt m v chnh xc hn so vi bilinear interpolation trong khng gian 3D.
